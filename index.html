My Journey

Who am I?

Byiringiro Frank, a Senior 4 MPC student from Ngororero District, Rwanda, is a passionate young programmer interested in web development, electronics (Arduino), and innovative community solutions. He aims to master React, JavaScript, PHP, and SQL to create impactful projects that bridge technology gaps in underserved communities.

My Biography

Born in 2007 in Ngororero District, Rwanda, Frank Byiringiro developed a passion for technology despite financial challenges. Now an entrepreneur, he creates affordable tech solutions for underserved communities while inspiring others through his journey of perseverance and innovation.

What I do

I specialize in full-stack web development and Arduino-based electronics projects. My services include building responsive websites, developing custom web applications, and creating innovative electronic solutions tailored to specific needs. I'm particularly interested in projects that combine both domains for smart systems.

Tikkun olam

As part of Tikkun Olam, I'm committed to using my skills to help others succeed academically. I mentor students in STEM subjects, particularly electronics and programming, sharing knowledge through workshops and one-on-one guidance. My goal is to create a ripple effect of learning and empowerment in my community.
