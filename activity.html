My Life Story

I was born with a curious mind and a passion for solving problems. From a young age, I found joy in learning how things work—whether it was fixing electronics, helping classmates with computers, or experimenting with science kits.

I joined boarding school, where I pushed myself to learn deeply—not just in class, but through real-world challenges. I became the president of the HeForShe club, a programmer in my school, and proudly represented my school in the STEM Day and science competitions. I even ranked among the top 100 students nationwide in the Rwanda Math Olympiad.

I’ve taught young children through Save the Children and helped them learn how to read and grow socially. These experiences shaped my belief that leadership is about service and impact.

Today, I’m combining tech and purpose to build smart systems—like a mosquito-zapping streetlamp powered by solar energy and a school dashboard that empowers students. My dream is to one day create innovations that serve Africa and the world.

— This is my journey. It's just the beginning.
