My Life Story

I was born in a small village in Ngororero District, raised by my mom alone. Life was tough, but it became the foundation of my determination. Growing up in that environment taught me resilience, hope, and the hunger to change my story through education and purpose.

Everything started to shift when I joined ASYV. There, I discovered a world of opportunities—learning electronics, design, and even the basics of mechanics. These new skills helped me realize who I truly am and opened my eyes to the potential within me.

Since then, I’ve become deeply involved in sharing what I’ve learned. I help others academically, offer guidance, and use what I know to make a difference. Whether through supporting classmates or building impactful projects, I strive to give back in meaningful ways.

From leading in the HeForShe club, programming school systems, joining elite electronics classes, to ranking among Rwanda’s top 100 in the Math Olympiad—I continue to grow with purpose. I dream of creating innovations that uplift communities in Africa and beyond.

— This is my journey. It's just the beginning.
